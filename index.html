About

I’m interested in how data and computation can help us design futures that are less extractive and more aligned with ecosystems. My work spans marine ecology, climate-driven change, and genomic structure, often with a focus on coastal systems and species that sit at the boundary of environment and industry.

I like projects that combine careful quantitative work with clear communication—whether that’s a time series model, a synteny plot, or a written review that makes complex systems feel legible.
